A 51-year-old man was shot in the chest by his 52-year-old roommate, Michael Lawrence Finnegan, during a dispute in Lucerne on Friday evening. The incident occurred around 8 p.m. at a residence on 15th Avenue, where Finnegan allegedly shot the victim with a .22-caliber pistol. The victim was hospitalized and is expected to survive. Finnegan claimed the victim had been hostile for several days, threatening to evict him and repeatedly entering his room. Feeling threatened, Finnegan said he shot his roommate in self-defense. He was arrested on suspicion of assault with a firearm.
